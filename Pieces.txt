Implements Drawable (does not extend GamePiece)
	Wall
Extends GamePiece, implements Moveable
	Warrior - moves back and forth, changing direction when it hits an object
	Sorcerer - moves randomly within a range of +/- 5 tiles
Extends GamePiece, with InteractionResult
	KILL - Warrior, interacts on same location
	HIT - Archer, hits with range +/- 3 tiles
	ADVANCE - Sorcerer, interacts on same location
	GET_POINT - Treasure, interacts on same location
